Tortelli di patate al ragù

Description

Tortelli di patate al ragù is one of the most beloved dishes of the cuisine from the Italian region of Tuscany

It is traditionally originated in the area near Florence called Mugello. These are also called tortelli alla Mugellana for that reason

Ingredients

For the tortelli

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Tomato sauce

For the ragù

  • Ground beef and pork
  • Salsicce
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery

Steps

  1. Boil some water and cook the potatoes
  2. Make the pasta for the tortelli by mixing flour and eggs
  3. Chop some onion, carrot and celery and cook them with some oil for 10 minutes
  4. Add the ground beef and pork and cook for 15 minutes
  5. Add the salsicce after chopping them with a cooking knife and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Add the tomato sauce and let cook for 3 hours
  7. Make the tortelli by cutting squares of pasta and closing them with a tablespoon of mushed potatoes inside
  8. Boil some water and cook the tortelli for 10 minutes
  9. Serve the tortelli with a generous amount of ragù
